About Quiz Night

Quiz Night is a popular pub quiz business that organizes fun and engaging quizzes for groups of friends and colleagues. With our extensive knowledge base and expert quiz masters, you can be sure that your quiz night will be a hit!

Whether you're looking for a casual night out or a fun team-building activity, Quiz Night has you covered. Our passion for trivia and commitment to creating an enjoyable atmosphere sets us apart.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by trivia enthusiast Jane Doe, Quiz Night has grown from a small local event to a beloved fixture in pubs across the city. Our mission is to bring people together through the joy of learning and friendly competition.
